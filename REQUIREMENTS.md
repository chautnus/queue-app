# Tai lieu Yeu cau Ung dung Hang Doi

> Nguon: https://docs.google.com/document/d/1i8L5RvPweLy01hARh-f9-q5s60ES3NlM/edit

## I. Gioi Thieu Chung

Du an nham xay dung mot ung dung di dong tao va quan ly hang doi cho khach hang, voi chuc nang danh cho ca admin va nhan vien phuc vu.

## II. Nen Tang Hoat Dong

- **Giai doan 1:** Ung dung Android (Google) — hien tai: PWA web
- **Giai doan 2:** Mo rong sang iOS sau khi tinh nang on dinh

## III. Chuc Nang Chinh

### 1. Xac Thuc va Dang Nhap (Man hinh 1)
- Dang nhap Google Account
- Dang nhap bang email
- Phan quyen cho admin va nhan vien

### 2. Tao Hang Doi (Man hinh 2)
Thong tin co ban can nhap:
- Ten hang doi
- Logo tren hang doi
- Thong diep danh cho khach hang

### 3. Cau Hinh Nang Cao (Man hinh 3)
- Thoi gian bat dau va ket thuc hang doi
- So luong (Flow) - mac dinh 1
- Neu so luong >1: ten luong, ten cua, thoi gian xu ly trung binh
- So luong cua phuc vu moi luong

### 4. Cai Dat Chung (Man hinh 4)
- Timezone
- Category: linh vuc cung cap dich vu (danh sach Google)
- Gio mo/dong cua
- Thong tin khach yeu cau: ten, so dien thoai, tuoi, dia chi
- Chuyen toi website khac (dat menu, tro choi, tuong tac)
- Tao ma QR code (co dinh hoac thay doi hang ngay)

## IV. Co Che Ky Thuat

### A. Quang Cao
- Tich hop adsense vao man hinh cho cua nguoi dung
- Ho tro cac co che quang cao khac

### B. Nhan Dang
- Kiem soat theo device ID
- Device moi: vao hang doi
- Device cu: hien thi so thu tu

### C. Ma QR
- Nhan vien: QR code de vao website phuc vu
- Khach hang: QR code de vao hang doi

## V. Giao Dien Quan Ly (Admin Dashboard)

### Thong Ke Hang Doi
- Tong so da dang ky
- Thoi gian phuc vu trung binh 1 khach hang

### Hang Doi Dang Chay
- Tong so da dang ky
- So dang phuc vu
- So dang cho theo luong
- Thoi gian du kien xu ly xong

## VI. Quy Trinh Khach Hang

### Khi Quet Ma QR
1. Xac minh la con nguoi (CAPTCHA)
2. Thu thap thong tin (neu yeu cau)
3. Hien thi so thu tu va ma kiem tra (4 so ngau nhien)
4. Mo tab moi neu tu dong chuyen trang
5. Yeu cau cho phep thong bao

### Landing Page Khach Hang
- Hien thi quang cao dau trang
- Thong tin hang doi
- So nguoi cho va thoi gian du kien
- Xac nhan tham gia
- Hien thi so thu tu va ma xac nhan
- Danh gia chat luong dich vu sau phuc vu

## VII. Quy Trinh Nhan Vien

### Quy Trinh Lam Viec
1. Quet QR code tai landing page
2. Dang nhap
3. Bat dau phien lam viec
4. Xac dinh quay (station) va danh muc phuc vu
5. Bat dau nhan khach
6. Nhap ma khach hang
7. Khach ke tiep
8. Tam nghi
9. Ket thuc phien

### Giao Dien Nhan Vien
- Uu tien giao dien dien thoai
- Nhap so thu tu + ma so khach
- Co the nhap 4 ma kiem tra (tuy cai dat)
- Hien thi so nguoi dang cho (theo luong)

## VIII. Bao Cao va Phan Tich

- Bao cao hien dang phuc vu theo nhan vien
- Xuat du lieu khach hang
- Bao cao cuoi ngay:
  - So luong khach phuc vu
  - Thoi gian phuc vu trung binh
  - Hanh vi nhan vien
